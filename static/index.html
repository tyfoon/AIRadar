How Privacy monitoring works

This tab combines two data sources for a complete privacy picture:
Zeek Network Analysis detects tracker connections at the TLS/SNI level — these are actual encrypted connections to ad networks, analytics platforms, and telemetry services. Even when your browser blocks cookies, these network-level trackers still phone home.
AdGuard Home blocks trackers at the DNS level — preventing the connection before it starts. Open AdGuard dashboard
